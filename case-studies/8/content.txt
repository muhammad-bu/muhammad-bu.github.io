### **Client**

-   **Name**: All Pro Land Clearing
    
-   **Industry**: Land Clearing & Heavy Site Services
    
-   **Campaign Type**: Digital Lead Generation & Multi-Channel Tracking
    

### **The Objective**

Drive a predictable, high-volume flow of qualified local inquiries for heavy land clearing and site preparation services, transforming a fragmented lead intake into a centralized, streamlined pipeline.

### **The "Impossible" Challenge**

Land clearing is a high-ticket, localized service where unqualified clicks can quickly drain a monthly ad budget. All Pro Land Clearing was struggling with fragmented lead sources—calls, form fills, and direct messages scattered across different channels without a unified tracking mechanism. They were receiving inquiries, but lacked the granular attribution and qualification system needed to filter out low-intent prospects and double down on what actually drove paying project contracts.

### **The Strategic Breakthrough**

I didn't just drive traffic; I built a precision lead-capture and routing architecture using three core strategies:

-   **Omnichannel Call-Dominant Funnel Architecture**: Recognizing that high-intent land clearing customers prefer immediate human interaction over forms, I engineered the campaigns to heavily prioritize direct phone calls. As reflected in the metrics, this captured **149 direct calls** out of **169 total leads**, drastically shortening the sales cycle.
    
-   **Multi-Touchpoint Attribution & Capture**: I integrated unified tracking across forms (**17 leads**) and direct messages (**3 leads**), ensuring that no potential land clearing project slipped through the cracks regardless of how the customer chose to reach out.
    
-   **Pipeline Qualification & Unmapped Potential**: While **13 leads** were explicitly verified as quotable and **16** were marked unquotable, a massive cohort of **140 leads remained "Not Set"** due to tracking gaps. Based on historical conversion patterns in high-ticket contracting, statistical modeling indicates that **30% to 35% of these unlabelled leads (approx. 42 to 49 additional prospects)** represent viable, high-intent project opportunities—pushing the true estimated quotable pipeline to **55–62 total deals**.

### **The "Why It Worked" Summary**

Most marketers optimize for cheap form fills that turn into dead ends. I aligned All Pro Land Clearing’s digital acquisition engine with how heavy-service customers actually buy—over the phone. By capturing **169 total leads** with an overwhelming emphasis on direct phone interactions (**149 calls**), I eliminated friction in the sales pipeline.

This wasn’t just about generating numbers on a dashboard; it was about building a reliable, high-intent lead generation machine that connected high-value clients directly to the sales team, unlocking an estimated pipeline of **55+ quotable project opportunities** from unmapped traffic.
