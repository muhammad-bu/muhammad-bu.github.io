
### **Client**

-   **Name**: All Seasons Excavating & Land Clearing
    
-   **Industry**: Excavation, Site Prep & Heavy Contracting
    
-   **Campaign Type**: Digital Lead Generation & Multi-Channel Acquisition
    

### **The Objective**

Engineered a high-performance lead generation engine to capture high-ticket excavation and site prep contracts, turning fragmented local search traffic into a steady stream of qualified project opportunities with massive pipeline value.

### **The "Impossible" Challenge**

In heavy contracting, low-intent inquiries can quietly drain a marketing budget. All Seasons Excavating needed a robust, systematic framework to capture high-value project leads, eliminate unqualified clicks, and attribute real revenue potential to their digital campaigns. They required precise visibility into lead types, conversion statuses, and total pipeline quote value to prove clear marketing ROI.

### **The Strategic Breakthrough**

I didn't just drive website traffic; I deployed an optimized, multi-channel capture and qualification system:

-   **Omnichannel Demand Capture**: I balanced direct phone calls (**116 calls**) with form submissions (**58 forms**), capturing **174 total leads** across the entire acquisition timeline. This dual-funnel approach ensured that whether clients preferred a quick phone consultation or a detailed digital project inquiry, no high-ticket lead slipped through the cracks.
    
-   **Massive Pipeline Generation ($2.59M Quote Value)**: By filtering for high-intent keywords and heavy-duty service queries, the campaigns generated an astonishing **$2.59M in Total Quote Value**, backed by **$121.10K in verified closed sales value**.
    
-   **High-Volume Qualification Potential**: Out of the total volume, **47 leads** were explicitly verified as quotable and **16** were marked unquotable. Meanwhile, **111 leads remained "Not Set"** in the tracking queue. Based on heavy contracting conversion metrics, a massive portion of these unassigned leads represent substantial untapped project pipeline value.
