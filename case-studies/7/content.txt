
### **Client**

-   **Name**: Capitol Tree Care
    
-   **Industry**: Arboriculture & High-Value Home Services
    
-   **Campaign Type**: Google Ads (Performance Search)
    

### **The Objective**

Transform a stagnant lead flow into a high-intent revenue engine in one of the most competitive home service markets.

### **The "Impossible" Challenge**

Capitol Tree Care faced a classic "scaling trap." In the high-stakes world of arboriculture, clicks are expensive, and generic lead generation strategies often result in low-value inquiries that waste budget. The client was bleeding cash on high-intent keywords that weren't converting into high-ticket jobs. They needed a surgeon, not a media buyer—someone to cut the waste and scale the revenue, not just the clicks.

### **The Strategic Breakthrough**

I didn't just "manage" campaigns; I engineered a system to hunt for profitability using three specific levers identified through a rigorous audit of **Campaign report (4).csv**:

-   **Intent-Based Bid Arbitrage**: I shifted focus away from generic "tree services" to high-conversion, long-tail search queries that signaled immediate project needs. By analyzing the data in **Time_series_chart(2025.11.19-2026.06.15).csv**, I identified a "Golden Window" for conversions—specific times of the day where purchase intent peaked. I aggressively bid up during these windows and decimated budgets on low-performing hours.
    
-   **Conversion Value Precision**: As seen in **image_663ffb.png**, we didn't aim for the lowest Cost Per Lead. We optimized for **Conversion Value**. I configured the engine to feed data back into Google’s machine learning to prioritize users who were likely to engage in large-scale tree removal and maintenance—services with a significantly higher lifetime value.
    
-   **Ruthless Efficiency**: I treated the ad account like a living organism. If a campaign or keyword didn't contribute to the $157,150 target, it was cut immediately. This aggressive pruning kept our conversion rate at a stable **2.91%** despite the increasing competitive pressure in the market.
